Agents do not need another lender asking for referrals.

They need useful buyer-facing tools and clearer market conversations. Buyer affordability confusion is real — grants, down payment assistance, VA zero-down financing, closing costs and credit requirements are genuinely confusing to explain in a single conversation, let alone a single showing.

Most buyers misunderstand what they qualify for, what a program actually covers, and what happens next. Agents feel that confusion every time a promising buyer stalls out. What agents need from a lending partner isn’t another pitch to send over more names — it’s a partner who can help educate the buyer before the transaction conversation even starts.

The lender who brings clarity becomes more useful.

Hero HomeReach gives lending teams a market education asset built specifically for Colorado’s veteran, teacher, first responder, healthcare and first-time buyer communities — content and campaigns a lender can put to work in their own agent relationships.
